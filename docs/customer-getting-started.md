# Customer Getting Started Guide

> For new healthcare organizations signing up for VistA Evolved.

## Welcome

Congratulations on choosing VistA Evolved! This guide walks you through
getting your organization set up and your first clinicians using the system.

## Step 1: Sign Up

1. Visit [vistaevolved.com/signup](https://vistaevolved.com/signup)
2. Select your organization type (Solo Clinic, Multi-Location, Hospital, etc.)
3. Choose your country and enter your contact email
4. Complete the 30-day free trial registration

After registration, you will receive a confirmation email with your tenant
credentials and admin login URL.

## Step 2: Administrator Setup

Your designated administrator will receive:

- **Admin Portal URL**: `https://<your-tenant>.vistaevolved.com/admin`
- **Initial admin credentials** (sent separately for security)

### First Admin Tasks

1. **Log in** to the admin portal with your initial credentials
2. **Change your password** on first login
3. **Configure your organization**:
   - Organization name and contact info
   - Facility/location details
   - Department structure
4. **Set up user accounts** for clinicians and staff
5. **Enable modules** relevant to your organization

### Module Options

| Module | Description | Typical Users |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| Clinical (CPRS) | Chart review, notes, orders, meds | All clinicians |
| Scheduling | Appointment management | Front desk, providers |
| Patient Portal | Patient self-service | Patients |
| Revenue Cycle | Billing, claims, coding | Billing staff |
| Imaging | PACS integration, radiology | Radiologists, techs |
| Telehealth | Video visits | Providers, patients |
| Analytics | Usage dashboards, reports | Administrators |
| Interoperability | FHIR, HL7 data exchange | IT staff |

## Step 3: Clinician Onboarding

### For Each Clinician

1. Admin creates their user account with appropriate role
2. Clinician receives login credentials via secure email
3. Clinician logs in and completes profile setup
4. Clinician accesses the CPRS Clinical Workspace

### Key Clinical Workflows

- **Patient Search**: Find patients by name, last 4 SSN, or ID
- **Chart Review**: Cover sheet shows allergies, vitals, problems, meds
- **Clinical Notes**: Create, sign, and manage progress notes
- **Orders**: Enter and manage lab, medication, and consult orders
- **Vitals**: Record and review vital signs

### Tips for Clinicians

- Use the CPRS panel navigation on the left to switch between views
- The Cover Sheet provides a quick clinical summary
- All changes are saved to VistA in real-time
- Notes require electronic signature before they appear in the chart

## Step 4: Patient Portal Setup

If your organization enabled the Patient Portal module:

1. Admin configures portal access settings
2. Patients receive invitation emails with secure registration links
3. Patients create accounts with identity verification
4. Patients can view records, request appointments, and message providers

### Patient Portal Features

- View allergies, medications, vitals, and lab results
- Request and manage appointments
- Send secure messages to care team
- Download immunization records
- Request prescription refills
- Complete intake forms before visits

## Step 5: Go-Live Checklist

Before going live with real patient data:

- [ ] All user accounts created and tested
- [ ] VistA connection verified (green status indicator)
- [ ] Clinical workflows tested with sandbox patients
- [ ] Billing/RCM configuration complete (if applicable)
- [ ] Patient portal tested end-to-end
- [ ] Staff trained on key workflows
- [ ] Support escalation path documented
- [ ] Data backup procedures confirmed

## FAQ

**Q: How do I connect to our existing VistA system?**
A: Contact support with your VistA host, port, and access details.
Our team will configure the secure connection.

**Q: Can we import data from another EHR?**
A: Yes. VistA Evolved supports FHIR R4, HL7v2, and custom migration.
Contact your account manager to discuss migration planning.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome 90+, Firefox 90+, Safari 15+, Edge 90+. Mobile browsers
are supported for the Patient Portal.

**Q: Is training available?**
A: Yes. All plans include documentation and video guides. Professional
and Enterprise plans include live training sessions.
