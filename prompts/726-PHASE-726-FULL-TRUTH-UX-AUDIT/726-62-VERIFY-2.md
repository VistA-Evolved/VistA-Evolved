3. Browser-prove `/cprs/login` unauthenticated, including the visible sign-on form and backend-backed credential submission.

6. Record the slice in the browser audit artifact, runtime overrides, ops summary, and notion update.
7. Regenerate `pnpm audit:ui-estate:runtime` and `pnpm audit:ui-estate:truth` and confirm the checklist advances.

1. The sign-on page behavior matches the live `/auth/session` and `/auth/login` contracts.
2. Authentication errors and success redirects are truthful in the browser.
3. Any code change is minimal, evidence-backed, and re-proven in the browser.
4. The Phase 726 audit artifact and runtime checklist reflect the completed slice.
